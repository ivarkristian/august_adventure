# August Playtest Rubric (v1)

August must score each dimension from 1 to 5 using these anchors.

## 1. Environment Richness

- 1: Flat and generic setting; little to notice.
- 2: Some distinct elements, but world still feels sparse.
- 3: Clear setting with a few memorable details.
- 4: Rich environmental texture that supports exploration.
- 5: Deeply layered world that rewards close attention.

## 2. Description Vividness

- 1: Confusing or bland room text.
- 2: Understandable but mostly plain descriptions.
- 3: Solid clarity; occasional evocative details.
- 4: Consistently vivid and easy to picture.
- 5: Highly evocative prose that strengthens atmosphere and play.

## 3. Puzzle and Challenge Quality

- 1: Arbitrary or unfair; little logical grounding.
- 2: Basic puzzle structure, but weak clues or pacing.
- 3: Mostly fair with understandable progression.
- 4: Well-paced, logical, and rewarding challenge design.
- 5: Elegant challenge arc with strong clueing and satisfying payoff.

## 4. Player Agency

- 1: Player choices feel narrow or mostly cosmetic.
- 2: Limited interaction set with frequent dead ends.
- 3: Reasonable action range with occasional friction.
- 4: Strong sense of control and meaningful options.
- 5: High freedom and consistently meaningful decision space.

## 5. World Coherence

- 1: Contradictory logic or inconsistent rules.
- 2: Repeated inconsistencies reduce trust.
- 3: Mostly coherent with minor rough edges.
- 4: Internally consistent world logic and responses.
- 5: Exceptionally coherent with robust causal consistency.

## 6. Curiosity and Engagement

- 1: Low motivation to continue.
- 2: Intermittent interest but weak momentum.
- 3: Steady engagement with some curiosity hooks.
- 4: Strong desire to explore and progress.
- 5: Compelling momentum with repeated surprise and delight.

## Scoring Requirements

- Always provide a short "why" for each score.
- Base scoring on observed transcript evidence.
- Do not invent alternative scales or half-step values.
- Overall score is the arithmetic average of all dimensions.
